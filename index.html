Let's cook some

GOOD FOOD

FOR 24/11 – 28/11

SUNDAY 24 DINNER
MONDAY 25 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): pasta, tuna, and sweet potatoes in mozzarella and tomato sauce. {Preheat the oven to 200°C, meanwhile cutting the potatoes into cubes. Once the oven is hot, put the potatoes in and boil the pasta both for 20 minutes. When they are close to being finished, fry the tuna for a few minutes on the pan, then mix in the rest of the ingredients and top it off with the tomato sauce. At the end once your food is on the plate, chuck in chunks of mozzarella so that it melts just with the temperature of the meal. Enjoy ❤}

MONDAY 25 DINNER
TUESDAY 26 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): chicken thighs, corn, and rice with mushrooms in honey BBQ sauce. {Preheat the oven to 200°C to cook the chicken for 1 hour. After around 40 minutes, add the mushrooms to the oven and boil the rice for the remaining 20 minutes, and towards the end of that boil another pot of salted water for the corn to cook it for 5 minutes. At the same time, in yet another pot, boil the honey with BBQ for the sauce. Enjoy ❤}

TUESDAY 26 DINNER
WEDNESDAY 27 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): chicken breast, sweet potatoes with mushrooms, sugar-snap peas, and mozzarella. {Put the potatoes in the microwave for 7 minutes to boil, cooking enough so that you can mash them later. For the chicken breasts use this recipe, but inside them also put mozzarella. At the end, bake the peas on a pan for around 5 minutes along with the mushrooms. At the end, mix the mushrooms into the mashed potatoes. Enjoy ❤}

WEDNESDAY 27 DINNER
THURSDAY 28 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): pasta, peppers, eggs, and cherry tomatoes in tomato sauce. {Boil the pasta for 20 minutes, and after 10 minutes on a pan cook the peppers and cherry tomatoes until soft. On a seperate pan, fry two eggs. Once your pasta is done, add tomato sauce to the pan with vegetables to mix them together. On the plate first put pasta, then the vegetable bolognese, and on top the two eggs and a dash of spring onions. Enjoy ❤}

SHOPPING LIST

  • sweet potatoes 1 bag
  • chicken thighs 1 pack
  • eggs 1 box
  • peppers 2 heads
  • mozzarella 2 packs
  • mushrooms 1 pack
  • tomato sauce 1 can
  • tuna 1 can
  • chicken breasts 1 pack
  • cherry tomatoes 1 pack
  • BBQ sauce 1 bottle
  • corn 1 packet (4 all together)
