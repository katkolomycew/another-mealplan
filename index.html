Let's cook some

GOOD FOOD

FOR 11/11 – 17/11

MONDAY 11 DINNER
TUESDAY 12 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): beef mince, rice, red kidney beans, chopped tomato purée, and peppers. {Prepare the vegetables - dice half of an onion and one pepper, then start with cooking these and adding spices (paprika/cumin/chilli). Next cook the beef in the same pan, and once cooked add the purée. After it boils together, add the beans. You can also use this recipe. Enjoy ❤}

TUESDAY 12 DINNER
WEDNESDAY 13 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): tuna, pasta, broccoli, and cherry tomatoes in carbonara sauce. {Boil the pasta and broccoli for 20 minutes, meanwhile cooking the tuna and cherry tomatoes on the pan. Once the pasta and broccoli are finished, add them to the pan along with the sauce, then cook for 5 more minutes. Enjoy ❤}

WEDNESDAY 13 DINNER
THURSDAY 14 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): beef mince, burger buns, lettuce, mozzarella, and sweet potato fries. {Preheat the oven to 200°C for the sweet potato fries, which later need to be cooked for 20 minutes. Meanwhile make burger patties with packets of mozzarella inside and cook them, then toast the buns and spread whichever sauce you want before adding lettuce and beef. Enjoy ❤}

THURSDAY 14 DINNER
FRIDAY 15 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): rice, tuna, broccoli, and cherry tomatoes in mozzarella. {Boil the rice and broccoli for 20 minutes, meanwhile cooking the tuna and cherry tomatoes together. At the end after mixing them together with the rice and broccoli, add a whole-ass hearty amount of cheese as if it were a sauce. Enjoy ❤}

FRIDAY 15 DINNER
SATURDAY 16 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): bacon, eggs, burger buns, mozzarella, and lettuce. {Cook the bacon with mozzarella on top once cooked, then make the eggs and add to the toasted patties with sauce and lettuce. Enjoy ❤}

SATURDAY 16 DINNER

Ingredients (5): sweet potatoes, cherry tomatoes, spring onions, chickpeas, and mozzarella. {Use this recipe, but in short preheat oven for the sweet potatoes and add the other ingredients with it to cook for 20 minutes. Enjoy ❤}

SHOPPING LIST

  • beef mince 1 pack
  • tuna 1 can
  • cherry tomatoes 1 pack
  • broccoli 1 head
  • carbonara 1 can
  • chopped tomato purée 1 can
  • pepper 1 red
  • bacon 1 pack
  • lettuce 1 pack
  • rice 1 pack
  • kidney beans 1 can
  • chickpeas 1 can
  • eggs 1 box
  • mozzarella 1 pack
  • burger buns 4 pieces
  • sweet potatoes 1 bag
  • spring onions 1 pack
  • onion 1 red
  • pasta 1 pack
