Let's cook some

GOOD FOOD

FOR 27/10 – 2/11

SUNDAY 27 DINNER
MONDAY 28 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): beef mince, rice, broccoli, and spring onions in carbonara sauce. {Get broccoli and rice cooking first in a pot for 20 minutes, meanwhile frying the onions then grilling the beef. Drain broccoli, add sauce to the onions and beef, and finish off by mixing broccoli to the rest of the pan. Enjoy ❤}

Snacks: yoghurt and cookies; ice cream and banana.

MONDAY 28 DINNER
TUESDAY 29 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): salmon, peas, mozzarella, mushrooms, and spaghetti. {Put the salmon in the oven for 22 minutes, meanwhile cooking the spaghetti in a pot and slicing the cucumber into thin stripes. When the salmon and spaghetti are ready, mix them and peas and mushrooms together in the pan. Sprinkle with mozzarella before serving. Enjoy ❤}

Snacks: yoghurt and cookies; ice cream and banana.

TUESDAY 29 DINNER
WEDNESDAY 30 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): chicken breast, sweet potatoes, broccoli, mozzarella, and spring onions. {Preheat the oven to 200°C and cut the sweet potatoes and chicken into big cubes. Put oil on sweet potatoes and stick in the oven for 30 minutes, meanwhile putting oil on the chicken. After these 30 minutes, take out the dish and add chicken, broccoli, spring onions, and mozzarella to bake for another 20 minutes. Enjoy ❤}

Snacks: yoghurt and peanut butter toast; strawberries and popcorn.

WEDNESDAY 30 DINNER
THURSDAY 31 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): beef mince, rice, peas, and mushrooms in tomato sauce. {Put the rice to boil for 20 minutes, meanwhile grilling the beef into burger-patty shapes and adding peas and mushrooms to the pan at the end. Once rice is cooked, add it to the pan to let everything cook together for a few minutes. Put everything on a plate, but leave the pan hot for cooking the tomato sauce separately, and later pour it over only the rice and beef. Enjoy ❤}

Snacks: yoghurt and peanut butter toast; strawberries and popcorn.

THURSDAY 31 DINNER
FRIDAY 1 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): chicken breast, spaghetti, spring onions, sugar-snap peas, and egg. {Boil the spaghetti for 20 minutes, meanwhile grilling the chicken. Once both are done, set them aside and fry the eggs. At the end, mix everything together in a pan except the eggs, which you can put on top before serving. Enjoy ❤}

Snacks: plain yoghurt with layered bananas and strawberries; toast with jam.

SATURDAY 2 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): chicken thighs, sweet potatoes, sugar-snap peas, egg, and mushrooms. {Preheat the oven to 200°C, meanwhile cutting the potatoes into flat wedges and grilling the peas for 2 minutes. Put the thighs in for 1 hour, taking them out after 40 minutes to also bake the potatoes and mushrooms for 20 minutes. Before you take everything out, fry the eggs. At the end, on a plate mix the rest of the ingredients with peas and eggs. Enjoy ❤}

Snacks: plain yoghurt with layered bananas and strawberries; toast with jam.

SATURDAY 2 DINNER

Ingredients (5): penne pasta, flour, milk, mozzarella, and spring onions. {Use this recipe for instructions, then just sprinkle with onions. Enjoy ❤}

Snacks: treat yourself to a whole tub of ice cream or cookies or whatever you feel like :)
