Let's cook some

GOOD FOOD

FOR 3/11 – 8/11

SUNDAY 3 DINNER
MONDAY 4 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): chicken breast, rice, broccoli, and mozzarella in carbonara sauce. {Put the rice and broccoli to boil for 20 minutes, meanwhile cutting up half of the packet of chicken breasts into stripes, then grilling the chicken. Once the rice and broccoli are finished, add them to the pan along with the cheese and sauce for 5 more minutes. Enjoy ❤}

MONDAY 4 DINNER
TUESDAY 5 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): beef mince, sweet potatoes, fried egg, and sugar-snap peas with breadcrumbs. {Peel the sweet potatoes, pierce small holes in them and microwave for 8 minutes. Meanwhile mix the beef into burger-patty shapes and grill them. Once the beef is cooked, throw the sweet potatoes onto the pan and mash them, afterwards cooking the peas for 2 minutes, then lay everything out on the plate before cooking and adding to the plate too. At the end, add plenty butter to the pan and add a few spoonfulls of breadcrumbs to pour them over the peas. Enjoy ❤}

TUESDAY 5 DINNER
WEDNESDAY 6 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): chicken breast, spaghetti, broccoli, and mushrooms in carbonara sauce. {Boil the pasta and broccoli for 20 minutes, meanwhile grilling the chicken and mushrooms, then shredding the chicken on the pan once cooked. Once the pasta and broccoli are finished, add them to the pan along with carbonara sauce and cook for 5 more minutes. Enjoy ❤}

WEDNESDAY 6 DINNER
THURSDAY 7 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): beef mince, tortilla wraps, rice, and mushrooms in tomato sauce. {Boil the rice for 20 minutes, meanwhile grilling the beef on a pan. Once the rice is cooked, add it along with the tomato sauce to the pan with the beef and also add the mushrooms to cook for 5 more minutes. On a separate pan, toast the tortilla before laying it on a plate and filling it with the rest of the ingredients. Enjoy ❤}

THURSDAY 7 DINNER
FRIDAY 8 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): sweet potatoes, sugar-snap peas, egg, and mozzarella in tomato sauce. {Preheat the oven to 200°C, meanwhile cutting the sweet potatoes into thick squares and later cooking them for 20 minutes. As you wait, grill the peas on a pan for 2 minutes and also fry the egg. At the end, add the sweet potatoes along with the tomato sauce and cheese to cook for 5 more minutes. Enjoy ❤}

SHOPPING LIST

  • beef mince 1 pack
  • chicken breasts 1 pack
  • rice 1 box
  • spaghetti 1 box
  • broccoli 1 head
  • sugar-snap peas 1 pack
  • mushrooms 1 pack
  • sweet potatoes 1 bag
  • eggs 1 box
  • mozzarella 1 packet
  • carbonara sauce 1 jar
  • tomato sauce 1 jar
  • breadcrumbs 1 packet
