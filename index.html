Let's cook some

GOOD FOOD

FOR 18/11 – 23/11

MONDAY 18 DINNER
TUESDAY 19 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): chicken wings, sweet potatoes, eggs, and sugar-snap peas in BBQ sauce. {Preheat the oven to 200°C. Put the chicken wings into a bowl so you can drizzle them with 2 tablespoons of oil, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper - get your hands dirty to even out the coating mix. Bake them for 40 minutes, then add sweet potatoes cut into fries for the last 20 minutes. While you wait these 20 minutes, cook the sugar-snap peas, fry the egg, and heat a pot so you can bring BBQ sauce and honey to a boil. Once the sauce boils, pour the mixture on the chicken wings. Enjoy ❤}

TUESDAY 19 DINNER
WEDNESDAY 20 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): ham, eggs, burger buns, mozzarella, and lettuce. {Cook the bacon with mozzarella on top once cooked, then make the eggs and add to the toasted patties with sauce and lettuce. Enjoy ❤}

WEDNESDAY 20 DINNER
THURSDAY 21 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): salmon, rice, broccoli, and sweet potato in carbonara sauce. {Preheat the oven to 200°C for the salmon and sweet potato (cut into squares/wedges) to bake for 22 minutes while at the same time boiling in a pot the rice and broccoli for 20 minutes. At the end, mix the rice, broccoli, and sweet potatoes on a heated pan for another 5 minutes. Enjoy ❤}

THURSDAY 21 DINNER
FRIDAY 22 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): pasta, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and mushrooms in tomato sauce. {Boil the pasta for 20 minutes, meanwhile cooking the cherry tomatoes and mushrooms on a pan for around 10 minutes. Once the pasta is finished, add it to the pan along with shredded mozzarella and the tomaot sauce, then cook for 5 more minutes so the mozzarella can melt and the tomato sauce can boil. Enjoy ❤}

FRIDAY 22 DINNER
SATURDAY 23 LUNCH

Ingredients (5): chicken wings, sweet potatoes, eggs, and mushrooms in tomato sauce. {Preheat the oven to 200°C. Put the chicken wings into a bowl so you can drizzle them with 2 tablespoons of oil, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper - get your hands dirty to even out the coating mix. Bake them for 40 minutes, then add sweet potatoes cut into fries for the last 20 minutes. While you wait these 20 minutes, cook the mushrooms, fry the egg, and heat a pot so you can bring tomato sauce to a boil. Once the sauce boils, pour the mixture on the chicken wings. Enjoy ❤}

SATURDAY 23 DINNER

Ingredients (6): salmon, pasta, broccoli, cherry tomatoes, and sugar-snap peas in carbonara sauce. {Preheat the oven to 200°C for the salmon to bake for 22 minutes while at the same time boiling in a pot the pasta and broccoli for 20 minutes. At the end, mix the pasta, broccoli, chery tomatoes, sugar-snap peas, and the carbonara sauce on a heated pan for another 5 minutes. Enjoy ❤}
